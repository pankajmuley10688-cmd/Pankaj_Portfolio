Citius BPO India Private Limited is a technology-driven Business Process Outsourcing organisation headquartered in Nagpur's prestigious MIHAN zone. The company specialises in IT infrastructure management, customer support, data processing, and back-office operations for global clients across multiple industry verticals.

With a state-of-the-art facility at Mihan W Building, 3rd Floor, near AIMS Hospital, Citius BPO provides a modern, collaborative work environment equipped with the latest technology stack — empowering teams to deliver consistent, high-quality outcomes for every client engagement.
